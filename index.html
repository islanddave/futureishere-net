Prompting FundamentalsSix techniques for getting better results from any conversation

📄A prompting reference doc is available now — Claude_prompting_guide.md. Full interactive guide cards coming soon.
Core Technique · 01

Be Clear & Specific

Front-load context, state the task upfront, and break large requests into concrete steps.

Coming Soon→
Core Technique · 02

Use Examples

Show Claude a sample of the format, tone, or style you want. A single reference output is worth a paragraph of abstract instructions.

Coming Soon→
Core Technique · 03

Encourage Step-by-Step Thinking

Ask Claude to "think through this step-by-step." Surfaces better answers on complex problems.

Coming Soon→
Core Technique · 04

Iterative Refinement

Specify exactly what to adjust — tone, length, a specific paragraph — rather than asking to "make it better."

Coming Soon→
Core Technique · 05

Leverage Claude's Knowledge

Provide context on how you'll use the answer. Claude's responses become far more targeted with downstream purpose.

Coming Soon→
Core Technique · 06

Role-Playing & Personas

Assign expert roles — CFO, negotiation coach, senior marketer — to shift the depth and intelligence of responses.
